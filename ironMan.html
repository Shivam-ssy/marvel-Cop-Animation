Iron-Man
Iron Man, played by Robert Downey Jr., is a cornerstone of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Tony Stark, the genius billionaire inventor, transforms himself into the armored superhero Iron Man. With his iconic red and gold suit, equipped with advanced technology, Stark fights villains and protects the world. Known for his witty banter, charisma, and personal growth, Iron Man's journey spans across multiple films, including the "Iron Man" trilogy, the "Avengers" series, and "Spider-Man: Homecoming." Robert Downey Jr.'s portrayal has made Iron Man an indelible and beloved character in the Marvel franchise.
