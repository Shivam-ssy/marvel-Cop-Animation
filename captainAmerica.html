Captain America
Captain America is a legendary Marvel character, known for his unwavering sense of justice and unyielding loyalty. Created during World War II, Steve Rogers, a frail young man, transforms into the super-soldier Captain America after an experimental serum. Armed with his iconic shield, he fights evil and stands as a symbol of hope and patriotism. As a founding member of the Avengers, he continues to inspire generations with his courage, leadership, and unbreakable spirit in the face of adversity.
